Developers

It’s no secret that everyone is looking for technical expertise to finesse their ventures or projects. The difference here is that we slim down the tide of requests into a select set of projects for you to scan through, once a month.



Lead by DoCSoc, Appsoc and Software Soc, we ensure the briefs contain actual insight into the vision of the project and the capabilities of the existing team – so you know exactly what you’re getting into.



You’ll receive monthly briefs on a highly filtered selection of ventures, stringently curated by Imperial Create Lab. All work is for cash or equity. No spam, no bullshit.

Ventures

The startup scene is bringing increasingly complex technology to market. Most ventures require a software or digital component and we’re trying to make the developer talent at Imperial more accessible.



‘CTO This’ filters for brilliant, well-thought through ideas and companies that have some of the pieces already, but need that touch of technical expertise to move to the next level. We help you find technical team members from amidst the morass of movement and noise that a complex ecosystem necessarily contains.


  1. Tell us a bit about your idea or venture and what you’re looking for

  2. We filter for commercially viable, non-exploitative and exciting projects

  3. Our community receives a monthly list of opportunities

  4. They reach out directly if they are interested

  5. We make sure everyone understands the terms of engagement

  6. It costs nothing. It’s a collaboration by organisations and individuals who benefit directly from its existence, you included
